## üéØ Brand Overview

### **Brand Name**
**Calcetix** - Beautiful calculator made simple

### **Tagline**
"Precision in every calculation"

### **Brand Mission**
To provide a beautiful, intuitive, and powerful calculator experience that makes mathematical calculations simple and enjoyable without complexity.

### **Brand Values**
- **Simplicity**: Clean, uncluttered design focused on calculations
- **Beauty**: Aesthetically pleasing interface that inspires confidence
- **Precision**: Accurate calculations with reliable performance
- **Accessibility**: Inclusive design for all users
- **Innovation**: Modern technology with thoughtful mathematical features

### **Target Audience**
- **Primary**: Students, professionals, and anyone who needs calculations (13-65 years)
- **Secondary**: Anyone who values beautiful, reliable tools
- **Characteristics**: Values simplicity, accuracy, and elegant design
